groupsif > 1 and trans == NoTranspose, smat is nRow x nCol with groups are vertically concatenated: each group is (nRow / groups) x nCol . if > 1 and trans == Transpose, smat is (nCol * groups) x (nRow / groups) with groups are horizontally concatenated: each group is nCol x (nRow / groups) . Each group is transposed and vertically concatenated to match with the NoTranspose case.
